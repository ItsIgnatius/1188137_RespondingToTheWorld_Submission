Two sides of the same coin

Before the Anthropocene, the earth and all living beings within it maintained a natural cycle of life.

Until human activity became the dominant influence on the earth's climate and environment, heavily affecting the ecosystem and distorting the cycle.

But just as a coin has two sides, humanity first destabilised the natural cycle, but are now actively working to provide an even greater, sustainable, and stable cycle of life.

And perhaps one day, with enough effort, such a harmonious world will be possible.
